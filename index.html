Our Story

BhatiaCards was founded in 1990 by Ravinder Bhatia with a vision to provide premium printing solutions that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation. Starting as a small neighborhood print shop, we've grown into a trusted name in the printing industry, serving clients across India.

Today, under the leadership of Mohit Bhatia, we continue to uphold the values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction that our founder instilled in the company. We blend time-honored techniques with cutting-edge technology to create products that celebrate your special moments.

Ravinder Bhatia

Founder (1990-Present)

Mohit Bhatia

CEO (2021-Present)

Our commitment to excellence has remained unchanged for over three decades. Each product that leaves our workshop carries the legacy of quality that the Bhatia name represents.
